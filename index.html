About us

At Doglife, we are on a mission to help street dogs in India. We are dedicated to improving the lives of these often-overlooked animals by providing resources and support for their care and adoption. Doglife assists people in locating all the NGOs in their cities, making it simple to find organizations that focus on rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming street dogs. Whether you are looking to adopt, volunteer, or donate, Doglife connects you with trusted local NGOs to ensure these dogs get the help they need. Together, we can make a meaningful impact and give street dogs the chance they deserve for a better life.
